    Things to Note:

    1. For DNA samples, send up to 1 μg or more of DNA in a maximum of 10 μL. Dissolved in TE Buffer or H2O.
    2. Raw samples (i.e. tissues or cell pellets) that will undergo RNA extraction and subsequent RNA sequencing must be suspended in Trizol.
    3. For Circular RNA Sequencing, send 5-10 μg of Total RNA in a maximum of 10 μL.
    4. For RNA Seq send 1 μg or more in a maximum of 10 μL.
    5. For pre-made deep sequencing libraries, please send us 12 μL (of 10nM if possible) along with the sequences of the barcodes.
